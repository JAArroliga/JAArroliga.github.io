Project Overview

Summary:

A fitness tracking website where users could input their diet and get nutritional information about it, create a custom exercise plan, and research exercises. The target audeience would be people who are new to fitness and be a resource to teach them about exercieses.

Site Map:

Page Designs:

  • Calculator: This will be a nutrition calculator. The customers will have to input the details of their meal and then the website will calculate macros, calories, and other nutritional information and compare them to the customer's goal. The webpage will need a couple of input boxes and possibly checkboxes and radio buttons for confirmation. When the user finally inputs all of the information and clicks submit their result should be displayed neatly and easily readable
  • Scheduler: A way for customers to schedule their workouts. The customers would be able to choose their exercise, put them on a schedule, and and then be able to export that schedule to a txt file. There would be a dropdown of the exercise and a way to input in time, reps, and weight. The user would then push a button to lock in the schedule and export to a txt file so they would be able to save it elsewhere.
  • Exercises: A Glossary of exercises. The customers would be able to click on a list of exercises and see an explination of how the exercise works and what to do. Includes a diagram/image example
  • Contact Us: Simple contact page. User would be able to click on the ECU Boxing social media accounts and be able to submit a question to to get more detailed information
